About FinCalcs

Simple, accurate financial calculators — nothing more, nothing less.

FinCalcs is a collection of free financial calculators for the United States and India. Every tool — from the mortgage and paycheck calculators to SIP, EMI and income tax calculators — runs entirely in your browser. There are no sign-ups, no spreadsheets to download, and the numbers you type never leave your device.

Why we built this

Most financial calculators online are buried in lead-capture forms, cluttered pages, or apps that want your phone number before showing a result. We wanted the opposite: type a number, move a slider, see the answer instantly — with the formula shown so you can verify the math yourself.

How the calculators work

Each calculator uses standard, published formulas — the amortization formula for loans and mortgages, compound interest for investments, and the current year's official tax slabs and brackets for the tax tools (2025 IRS brackets for the US; FY 2025-26 new and old regimes for India). Results are cross-checked against leading reference calculators. That said, these tools provide estimates for education and planning — they are not financial, tax or legal advice. Please consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.
