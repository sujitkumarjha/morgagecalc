FAQ

Which regime should I pick?

The new regime usually wins unless your total deductions are large (typically ₹4L+ at higher incomes). This calculator shows the exact break-even for your numbers — just move the deductions slider.

Is surcharge included?

Surcharge applies above ₹50L total income (10%–25%). This tool targets typical salaried incomes and excludes surcharge; treat results above ₹50L as indicative.
